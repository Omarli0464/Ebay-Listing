Description

This Onkyo SACD plays CDs and SACDs, and sounds great. I've been the only owner and I've been very happy with it, with one exception. At some point the eject functionality started to get spotty. So while disks load and play fine, sometimes when you try to eject the disk, it will not eject. The workaround is to open the unit (via screws on the bottom) and manually remove the disk, but obviously this is not ideal. I don't want to deal with getting it repaired, but it could be a small thing that someone with some skills may be able to fix it for free. The top has a some scratches that you can see in the photos, but nothing major, and none on the front. Remote, power cord and original packaging included.

Includes:

  • Onkyo C-S5VL player
  • Onkyo C-S5VL remote
  • Power cord
  • Original packaging

Again unit functions and plays fine, however the eject functionality will intermittently fail to work. See description.
